A Deeper Look at Rome

As you experience your trip in Italy is important to take note of some of the most important stops in one of Italys most important cities. Rome! Rome is a beautiful city in Italy that is known for its history in art, architecture, cuisine and much more.

What To See?

Rome is home to some of the most famous historical monuments in Italian history as well as global history. It has sites such as the Collosseum, the Roman Forum, the Trevi Fountain, and the Vatican.

How Long to Stay and When to Go?

It is important that when you plan your trip to Italy you take into account the best times of the year to see a place like Rome. Rome is a walking city so its would be best to go during the spring and autumn seasons. Romes summers can be hot and Romes winters can be cold. Most recommend that you stay atleast 4 days in Rome to experience the city fully.
